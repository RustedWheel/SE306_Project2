Welcome to Zorg-Zorg. 
This is a planet with truth and deceit. 
Be wary of what others tell you and make the correct decisions. 
If you would like to talk to other aliens, press [space] when you are near them.
Any items you walk by will be automatically stored in your item inventory, to open your inventory, press [i].
You can select and see the descriptions of any item by clicking on it.
To review any conversation you have had with any alien, press [c].
Don't worry, you shall soon get used to exploring around in Zorg-Zorg.
